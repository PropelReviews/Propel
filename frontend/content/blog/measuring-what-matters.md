date: 2026-07-15
description: A short note on defining success metrics with your team, not for them.

---

Vanity metrics are easy. Useful ones take conversation.

The best engineering dashboards start with a shared definition of success —
cycle time that matches how you ship, review load that reflects your process,
and tooling signals that answer real questions.

Propel is built around that idea: define the metric with your team, then keep
the pipeline transparent so everyone can trust the number.
